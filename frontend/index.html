What is depression and what can YOU do about it?

Depression is the leading cause of disability worldwide, according to the World Health Organization (WHO). It can undermine a person’s relationships, make working and maintaining good health very difficult, and in severe cases, may lead to suicide. In fact, depression contributes to nearly 40,000 suicides in the United States each year. It can affect adults, adolescents, and children.
